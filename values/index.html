Values and Vision

We strive to provide personalized, hands on consulting, facilitation, training and coaching to help ensure the greatest impact of community resources on community challenges.

Our Values

Focus:  Plans and people are focused on making the greatest impact with their resources and the right strategy.

Positive Relationships:  Good plans require people working together and trusting one another to implement positive change – to do what is best for constituents served and the organization.

Partnerships:   No single organization can do everything, so inclusive collaborations among stakeholders for ‘win-win’ scenarios is needed for effective change.

Reliability: All parties involved do what they say they will do.

Perseverance: Despite challenges and obstacles, we all strive to meet our commitments.

Respect:  We value and respect those served and provide better service than anticipated.

Transparency:  Effective relationships and partnerships require open and honest communication.

Mediation/Facilitation: Work through conflicts in a positive, meaningful way.
